About Us

EFI Pro Painting

EFI ProPainting is a full-service painting contractor specializing in interior & exterior painting and waterproofing of residential & commercial properties.

With a presence in the South Florida market for 24 years, in addition to residential repaints, we specialize in painting high-rise and mid-rise buildings, townhouse and condominium projects, apartment communities, shopping centers, office building, hotels and hospitals.

About Us

A Message from the Owner

“I would like to take this opportunity to introduce EFI ProPainting to you. EFI ProPainting specializes in the preparation and painting of interiors and exteriors of all types of buildings and single-family residences. We also specialize in painting and waterproofing of high-rise buildings, condominiums, apartment complexes, shopping centers, hospitals, and commercial buildings.”

“I am personally involved in every aspect of your project. With over 24 years of paint contracting experience, you can rest assured that your job is my top priority from start to finish. In order to enforce and maintain our strict policy of quality control, all of my projects are started and completed by the same foreman and crew.

This is in addition to my personal supervision of every aspect of your job. I guarantee that your job is completed to my high standards. When it comes to materials, we only use the best, top-line products from national manufacturers like Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, ICI, and PPG/Porter Paints. And of course, I am licensed and insured for everyone’s protection. I strive to provide my customers with a sense of security by knowing that my reputation, skill, experience, and knowledge will get the job done right!”
